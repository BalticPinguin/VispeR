Introduction

The output of a Visper-Calculation shows some general information about the calculation performed. Depending on the details level chosen, most of these data are not calculated inside that programme but are imported from the log-files given.

The information given can be divided into three kinds:

  • Static output
  • More or less detailed information about the performed calculations
  • Informations about the calculation itself such as warnings, errors or similar information.

Units: energy is given in cm-1, coordinates in Angstroem, masses in amu.
